Departments

Health

A health department is a government agency, operating at local, state, or national levels, tasked with protecting and promoting the health of the general public. Acting as the foundation for public health, these departments focus on preventing disease, managing health policies, and ensuring environmental safety. Their core functions include monitoring community health status, investigating health hazards, enforcing safety regulations, and providing essential services such as immunizations and maternal care. By addressing both communicable and noncommunicable disease risks, health departments work to improve overall population well-being, often acting as a safety net for vulnerable, uninsured, or underserved populations

Within the department, there are various civil servants whose salary is determined by their medical professions, as shown below:

    Professionals and their Standard Pay

    1.Medical doctors and Specialists

  • Medical Officer Intern: Approx. KES 68,000 – KES 100,000+ per month.
  • Medical Officer (Entry Level/JG M): Approx. KES 206,000 – KES 413,000+ (depending on facility level, including allowances).
  • Senior Consultant/Doctor: Up to KES 1.4 million per month.

    • 2.Clinical Officers

  • Clinical Officer Intern (Diploma): KES 27,000 – KES 35,000 per month.
  • Registered Clinical Officer II (Diploma): ~KES 35,000 – KES 150,000 per month.
  • Senior Clinical Officer/Specialist: ~KES 150,000 – KES 338,000+ per month.

    • 3.Nurses

  • Nursing Officer Intern (Degree): KES 35,000 – KES 50,000 per month.
  • Enrolled Nurse III (JG G): ~KES 22,000 – KES 30,000+ (basic + allowances).
  • Registered Nurse III (JG H): ~KES 39,000 – KES 45,000+ (basic + allowances).
  • Senior/Chief Registered Nurse (JG L-M): ~KES 50,000 – KES 60,000+.
  • Senior Principal Nurse (JG P): KES 85,000 – KES 120,000+.

    • 4.Public Health and Technical Staff

  • Community Health Assistant/Worker (Entry): ~KES 26,900 – KES 45,000+ per month.
  • Public Health Officer/Technician (Diploma/Degree): ~KES 35,000 – KES 120,000+
  • Epidemiologist/Disease Surveillance Officer: ~KES 90,000 – KES 200,000+.
  • Medical Laboratory Technologist: ~KES 360,000 – KES 723,000 annually (Avg. KES 30k–60k+ monthly).
  • Health Records & Information Officer: ~KES 40,000 – KES 60,000 (Entry).

    • 5.Administrative and Other Support Staff

  • Health Administrator: ~KES 117,000 – KES 173,000+
  • Accountant (Health Department): ~KES 413,000 – KES 617,000 annually.
  • Mortuary Attendants/Technicians: ~KES 20,000 – KES 40,000+ (varies widely).
